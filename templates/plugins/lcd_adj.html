LCD settings

This plugin sends data to I2C for LCD 16x2 (16x1) char with PCF8574(A). Automatically detects i2c at: 0x20-0x27, 0x38-0x3F. Compatible with HD44780 controller.

Use I2C LCD:
2 or 1 line display (default 2 line display)
